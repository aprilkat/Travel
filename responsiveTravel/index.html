Travel to Japan

Traveling to Japan is an amazing experience. There are many exiting places to visit and see. If you want to experience a foreign culture, than Japan is the perfect place to travel to. Japan is an island country located in the Pacific Ocean. The weather conditions range from sub-tropical to cold depending on what part of the country you visit. Nearly three-quarters of Japan's terrain is mountainous.

Mt. Fuji is a stunning sight. Located near Fujinomiyo, Japan. It draws many tourists from around the world.

Yokohama, Japan is second only to Tokyo in size. It is a very colorful and beautiful city. It is known for it's abundance in sightseeing spots. This is a beautiful picture of the night lights of Yokohama.

All cities in Japan are known for being bright and colorful. They display their unique culture all over buildings and down every street.

Tokyo, Japan is the largest city and capital of Japan. It is also one of the most well-known Japanese cities in the world. It is a mix of modern and ancient and it is a stunning sight.

Japan is a unique country. Everything from their food to their art and music can only be found there. The Japanese culture has spread to many other countries, some is very subtle and hard to notice, while some is very obvious.
